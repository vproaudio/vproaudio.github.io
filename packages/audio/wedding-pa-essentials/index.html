Package overview

The Wedding PA Essentials package is the perfect audio solution for your special day, covering everything from the ceremony to the reception. It includes two powerful 1000W speakers, a mixer, and a wireless microphone—ideal for officiants, speeches, and announcements. With Bluetooth connectivity, you can easily connect your phone or tablet to play music for the ceremony, cocktail hour, and reception. Compact, reliable, and easy to use, this package ensures clear, professional sound throughout your entire wedding celebration.
