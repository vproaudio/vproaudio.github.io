Package overview

Bring the fun and energy of a karaoke night to your next event with our Karaoke Pro Pack. This all-in-one rental package is perfect for birthdays, backyard parties, weddings, corporate gatherings, and more. It includes two powerful 700-watt speakers that deliver clear, room-filling sound, along with a professional mixer featuring built-in vocal effects like echo and reverb to enhance your performance. A high-quality wireless microphone gives you the freedom to move around as you sing, while the included iPad—loaded with Spotify and YouTube Premium—gives you instant access to thousands of karaoke tracks with no ads or interruptions. To complete the setup, we provide a sturdy mic stand with an integrated iPad holder for hands-free convenience. Whether you’re a seasoned singer or just want to have fun with friends, the Karaoke Pro Pack makes it easy.
