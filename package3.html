Package 3

This package enhances Package 1 by adding a subwoofer, delivering the powerful low-end punch your sound system deserves. This is the go to package if you need to mostly play music for your audience. A wired vocal microphone package (microphone, stand and cable) is included with this package in case you need to make announcements between playing music.

Includes

  • 1 x 1000W Full Range Powered Speaker
  • 1 x 1020W Powered Subwoofer
  • 1 x Subwoofer to Speaker Pole
  • 1 x Wired Vocal Microphone
  • 1 x 25 ft XLR Microphone Cable
  • 1 x Microphone Stand

Features

  • Bluetooth channel for playing audio from your phone/tablet
  • Two XLR and 1/4 inch TS inputs allowing you to connect one microphone and one instrument or two microphones
  • Hi-Z inpput for direct guitar connections
  • 3-Band and Parametric EQ for sound enhancement
  • Built in effects such as reverb to further enhance your sound

Perfect For

  • Backyard parties
  • Corporate parties and events
  • Karaokes
  • DJs

Max System Power

  • 2020 Watts

Upgrades

  • Replace wired vocal microphone with a wireless vocal microphone for an extra $20 per day
