Package 1

This is our most basic package which includes all accessories you need to get started with your event.

Includes

  • 1 x 1000W 12-inch Full Range Powered Speaker
  • 1 x Tripod Speaker Stand
  • 1 x Wired Vocal Microphone
  • 1 x 25 ft XLR Microphone Cable
  • 1 x Microphone Stand
  • 1 x 25 ft 1/4 inch Guitar Cable (optional)

Features

  • Bluetooth channel for playing audio from your phone/tablet
  • Can connect one microphone and one instrument
  • Hi-Z inpput for direct guitar connections
  • 3-Band and Parametric EQ for sound enhancement
  • Built in effects such as reverb to further modify your sound

Perfect For

  • Solo performances
  • Backyard gatherings
  • Small company meetings
  • Exhibitor presentations

Total System Power

  • 1000 Watts

Options

  • Replace wired vocal microphone with a wireless microphone for an extra $30 per day
